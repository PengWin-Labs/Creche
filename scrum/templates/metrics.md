## Notes

- **Velocity:** story points completed (not committed)
- **Goal Met:** Y = fully met · P = partially met · N = missed
- **Carryover %:** committed points that slipped ÷ committed total
- **Experiment:** brief note on retro experiment result (✓ kept / ✗ dropped)
