## Overview

Forage is the parallel SaaS product under [[Pengwin Labs]] LLC, distinct from [[Crèche]]. Revenue-generating. **Functions as a paid training ground for capabilities Crèche needs.** Out of scope for the Crèche wiki except where it intersects business and legal.

## Naming

"Forage" — going out to find what's needed. Targeted, applied work. Follows the [[Pengwin Labs]] naming convention (penguin behavior + human meaning + role-fit).

## Why a Parallel Product

Crèche is mission-driven, free, AGPL-3.0. Forage is commercial. Building the commercial product alongside the mission product produces:

- **Revenue** to fund Crèche development without compromising Crèche's free-and-open commitment
- **A faster feedback loop** for capabilities Crèche eventually needs — Forage uses them under load before Crèche depends on them
- **Liability separation** (once Series LLC amendment lands)

## Origin

Built around a family member's business needs. The capabilities Forage develops translate to Crèche but the user base, pricing model, and mission are different.

## Relationship to Crèche

- **Crèche** — mission product; free; AGPL-3.0; user data lives in user-owned repos; Pengwin Labs cannot read it
- **Forage** — commercial product; paid; standard SaaS terms; capabilities mature here first

The two products share infrastructure (Pengwin Labs LLC, GitHub org, banking) but not data, not licensing, not mission framing.

## Status

Not the focus of this wiki. Detailed design lives elsewhere. References here exist because [[Pengwin Labs]] structure depends on the Series LLC amendment that includes Forage as the secondary series.
